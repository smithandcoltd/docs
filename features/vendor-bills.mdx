title: "Vendor bills"
description: "How local and synced vendor bills are displayed, linked, approved, and reconciled"
---

The **Vendor Bills** tab is an admin-only internal surface for bill review and reconciliation.

## Access

Only users with bill-modification permission can view the tab. In practice, that means admins. Non-admin users are redirected back to the Draws tab.

## Why the page exists

The page brings together two kinds of bill records:

- local bill records created through draw approval
- synced JobTread vendor bills that already exist in the source system

That is why this page is mainly a reconciliation and follow-through page, not the place where the original draw is assembled.

## Unified bill model

The UI merges:

1. local draft and workflow-linked bills from Supabase
2. JobTread bills returned by `fetch-invoice-schedule`

Important behaviors:

- synced JobTread IDs are tracked to avoid duplicate display
- older local duplicates without JobTread links can be suppressed when a synced sibling exists
- orphan JobTread bills still appear even when not linked to a local draw

## Sync states

| Sync state | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| `pending_sync` | Local-only bill not yet synced or linked |
| `synced` | Local bill is linked to a JobTread bill |
| `missing_in_jobtread` | Local record expects a JobTread bill that is not present in the current sync |
| `orphan_jobtread` | JobTread bill exists without a local portal draw link |

## Available actions

- sync bills from JobTread
- link unlinked bills to a local draw
- approve bills
- revert bills to pending
- delete bills in allowed workflow states
- generate draw PDFs or DOCX outputs
- generate waivers

## What approval means here

Approving a bill from this tab does two things:

1. creates the vendor bill in JobTread
2. updates the local bill status to `approved`

Reverting an approved bill does the reverse workflow: the app voids the JobTread bill and returns the local record to `pending`.

## Linking unlinked bills

The link dialog only offers:

- bills without a linked local draw
- bills that already have a JobTread bill ID
- local draws in `ready` or `paid` state

This is how the app reconnects a JobTread bill back to a local draw record when the link was missing.

The dialog intentionally ignores bills that are already linked or bills that do not yet have a JobTread bill ID.

## Restricted-code handling

Restricted bill items are filtered out of some display and matching flows so the bills tab reflects the admin-only visibility model correctly.

## Waivers

Waiver records are local Supabase records tied to draws and, when available, bills.

The waiver tracking layer stores:

- waiver type
- status
- amount
- through date
- file URL
- approval timestamps
