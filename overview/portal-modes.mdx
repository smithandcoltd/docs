---
title: "Portal modes"
description: "How dashboard mode flags change tabs, visibility, and approval side effects"
---

Each dashboard is controlled by two flags stored on the dashboard record:

- `minimal_portal`
- `show_invoice_draw`

Those two flags decide both:

1. what the client-facing dashboard looks like
2. which internal finance workflow the team uses

## Mode summary

| Mode | Trigger | Client-facing dashboard tabs | Internal invoice-draw tabs | Approval side effect |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Standard draw | `minimal_portal = false` and `show_invoice_draw = true` | Contractor Summary, Change Orders, Specifications | Schedule, Draws, Vendor Bills | Creates a JobTread customer invoice and local pending bill records |
| Hide financials | `minimal_portal = false` and `show_invoice_draw = false` | Change Orders, Specifications | Schedule, Draws, Vendor Bills | Still creates a JobTread customer invoice and local pending bill records |
| 40-40-20 sign-off | `minimal_portal = true` | Invoices, Change Orders, Specifications | Draws, Vendor Bills, Invoices | Skips JobTread customer invoice creation but still creates local pending bill records |

## Mutual exclusion rules

- 40-40-20 mode takes precedence over hide financials.
- When `minimal_portal` is turned on, the app forces `show_invoice_draw` back to `true`.
- So a dashboard cannot stay in both modes at the same time.

## What changes in the UI

### Standard draw

- Clients can see the contractor summary financial view.
- Internal users get the full invoice-draw workflow.
- The default invoice-draw tab is **Schedule**.

### Hide financials

- Clients lose the contractor summary tab, but they still see non-financial project pages.
- Internal users still keep the full invoice-draw workflow.
- If someone tries to open a hidden tab directly, the route redirects them to an allowed tab.

### 40-40-20 sign-off

- The dashboard page shows **Invoices** instead of **Contractor Summary**.
- The internal invoice-draw page removes **Schedule**.
- The default invoice-draw tab becomes **Draws**.
- Approval behaves like sign-off on a package, not standard invoice creation.

## Why this matters

Portal mode changes more than navigation labels. It changes:

- which financial details clients are allowed to see
- which workflow the internal team is supposed to follow
- whether approval should create a JobTread customer invoice
- which feature docs apply to the page you are looking at
